Frequently Asked Questions

How often is new content added?

We regularly update the app with new content, including additional vocabulary words, grammar lessons, and game features. Updates are typically released every 1-2 months.

Can I use Yossr on multiple devices?

Currently, Yossr stores all data locally on each device. To use the app on multiple devices, you'll need to download it separately on each device and start fresh on each one.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely! Yossr stores all your data locally on your device. We don't have servers that collect your personal information. The only external data collection happens through AdMob ads. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

How can I suggest new features or report bugs?

We welcome all feedback! Please email us at yossr.school@gmail.com with your suggestions or bug reports. We read every message and appreciate your input in making Yossr better.
