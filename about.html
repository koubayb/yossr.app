About Yossr

We believe that learning English should be engaging, effective, and accessible to everyone. Yossr combines gamification with proven learning methodologies to create an unforgettable language learning experience.

Our Mission

To make English learning accessible, enjoyable, and effective for everyone through innovative gamification and comprehensive educational content. We're committed to breaking down language barriers and empowering learners worldwide.

Our Vision

We envision a world where language learning is no longer a chore but an exciting journey. Where millions of learners achieve fluency through engaging experiences that make education feel like entertainment.

What Makes Yossr Unique

Gamified Learning

Transform vocabulary building into an exciting game with levels, rewards, and daily challenges that keep you motivated.

Oxford-Based Content

Learn the 5000+ most commonly used English words curated from Oxford dictionaries for practical, real-world usage.

Comprehensive Grammar

From basic tenses to complex structures, all grammar lessons are designed specifically for English learners at every level.

Our Story

Founded by language enthusiasts and education technology experts, Yossr was born from a simple observation: people learn better when they're having fun.

After years of research in language acquisition and gamification, our team set out to create an English learning app that would truly engage users while delivering measurable results.

We combined the most effective learning techniques with engaging game mechanics, ensuring that every session with Yossr feels both enjoyable and educational. Our content is carefully curated from Oxford's research on the most commonly used English words, making your learning journey efficient and practical.

Today, Yossr continues to evolve with regular updates, new features, and expanded content to serve our growing community of English learners worldwide.
